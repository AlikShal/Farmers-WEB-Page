Project description

This midterm project (Farmers Market Managing System) targets market managers, vendors, and local customers. Purpose: provide a simple UI to track vendors, display products, collect feedback, and produce basic sales reports. The goal is an easy-to-maintain site with clear navigation and minimal complexity for deployment (GitHub Pages / Netlify).

Sitemap

  1. Home — dashboard & quick stats
  2. About — tribute and project overview
  3. Vendors — vendor list & contact info
  4. Products — product catalog & sales table
  5. Feedback — form & feedback management

Wireframe notes (textual)

Header: logo/title + top nav

Content area: main area with cards, tables or forms depending on page

Sidebar: quick links and statistics (on home page it appears as left column)

Footer: contact info & copyright

Wireframes can be sketched in Figma; here we provide layout notes for defense — each page has consistent header/footer/navigation, as required.
